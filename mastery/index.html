Why Mastery Matters

Mastery is not reserved for genius. Robert Greene distills centuries of wisdom—from Darwin to Mozart to Einstein—into six phases: Life's Task, Apprenticeship, Mentor Dynamic, Social Intelligence, Creative-Active, and Mastery. Desire, persistence, and confidence drive the path more than raw reasoning.

The Six-Part Framework

A structured path from discovering your calling to fusing intuition with reason.
