What This Site Is

This is an informational and reflective presentation of Robert Greene's The Art of Seduction. It is not a guide to manipulation or coercion. Rather, it is a structured reading guide—a framework for understanding charm, theatricality, persona, desire, and the psychology of attraction. Read it as analytical literature, not as interpersonal advice.

The Seductive Types

Nine archetypes that recur across history and culture. Each represents a pattern of fascination—a way desire and attention are structured.
