What This Book Is About

Power is not random. Across cultures and centuries, the same patterns repeat: those who understand them advance; those who ignore them fall. Robert Greene distills 3,000 years of history into 48 laws—principles of strategy, perception, and influence that remain as relevant today as in the courts of Renaissance Europe.
